EE2201 - Circuit Theory

Credit points: 03
Year: 2024
Student Contribution Band: Band 2
Prerequisites: EG1012 and MA2000. Allow concurrent enrolment for MA2000.
Administered by: College of Science and Engineering

Subject Description

    AC circuit theorems and network analysis. Sinusoids and phasors. Frequency response, transfer function, stability, Bode diagrams, locus diagrams, resonance. Two port networks. The Laplace transform, transient analysis using the Laplace transform.

Learning Outcomes

  • to demonstrate competence in the application of available techniques including mesh and nodal analysis; circuit reduction techniques; frequency response characteristics; two-port network parameters; transient analysis

Subject Assessment

  • Written > Examination (centrally administered) - (60%) - Individual
  • Written > Examination - In class - (15%) - Individual
  • On-course assessment: Consisting of two assignments worth 10% each and Practicals worth 5% - (25%) - Individual
